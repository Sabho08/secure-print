Application Name: SecurePrint
Tagline: Secure Printing. Redefined.

Concept & Context:
SecurePrint is a premium, high-security document management and printing platform designed for modern corporate environments (Trusted by Fortune 500). The core value proposition is "Zero-Footprint" secure printing—ensuring that sensitive documents are never permanently stored on servers but are instead encrypted and streamed directly to a secure print terminal.

Key Themes for Logo Design:
1. Security & Protection: The logo should evoke trust and military-grade safety. Common symbols: Shield, Lock, or stylized "Secure" enclosure.
2. Speed & Efficiency: Despite being high-security, the process is seamless and fast. Common symbols: Bolt, streaming lines, or dynamic geometric shapes.
3. Modern & Professional: The aesthetic is clean, minimalist, and "Enterprise-Ready." It should look at home in a high-end corporate office.
4. Connectivity: Integration with major cloud services (Google Drive, Microsoft 365) and real-time streaming between devices.

Visual Style:
- Color Palette: Deep professional blues (#0b55cf), combined with clean white/gray surfaces and subtle tech gradients.
- Typography: Strong, bold, sans-serif font (Inter/Helvetica style) that conveys stability and authority.
- Iconography: A fusion of "Security" (Shield/Lock) and "Document/Print" (Paper/Tray) elements.

Logo Design Prompt for Stitch:
"A professional, minimalist logo for 'SecurePrint'. The icon should feature a sleek, modern shield integrated with a document or a print-tray silhouette, conveying security and efficiency. Use a deep blue and tech-blue gradient palette. The style should be clean, high-contrast, and suitable for a tech-focused corporate application. Avoid clutter; focus on bold, recognizable geometry."
